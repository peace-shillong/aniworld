Gold Fish

The Goldfish (Carassius auratus) is a freshwater fish in the family Cyprinidae of order Cypriniformes. It is one of the most commonly kept aquarium fish.

Learn More

The goldfish is native to East Asia, including China and perhaps adjacent regions (Japan, Republic of Korea).

There are various variety of goldfish:

  • Common goldfish
  • Comet
  • Oranda
  • Lionhead
  • Egg-fish goldfish
  • Curled-gill goldfish
